chi_crashes_2019.geojson, traffic crashes in Chicago (2019), processed and downloaded from https://data.cityofchicago.org/Transportation/Traffic-Crashes-Crashes/85ca-t3if

data with null or zero latitude and longitude are geocoded based on street addresses using ESRI World Geocoding Service. Columns Status, Score, and Match_addr geocoding methods, score and matched addresses. 

data with non-null latitude and longitude are merged with geocoded data above

some inconsistent rows were removed
